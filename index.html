THE COMPRESSED STORY

DVD (pronounced "David") Goodwin, pronouns he/him/his or they/them/their.

DVD is a dynamic professional with a unique journey from pastoral care to corporate innovation. He is currently enrolled full-time at Grand Circus Coding Bootcamp, studying full-stack C#/.net/Java in order to launch a career in software engineering.

BLOGGING

Ever since DVD can remember, they wrote fiction for a hobby.
Was it good fiction? Who knows. In high school, he wrote a serial murder mystery about a high school sleuth named Lewis Beacon.
After college, he organized a website called HOW WE LOST THE MOON, publishing submissions both from DVD and friends every new moon.
Today, some of DVD's contributions can be found on their medium blog.
Whether such a hobby will survive writing code for a career is yet to be seen, but no matter what, DVD remains forever a writer.

DEVELOPMENT

Now that DVD is in Grand Circus, all of his writing has been in an IDE (Integrated Development Environment).
Looking over his GitHub, you will find a progression in programs, from a simple inventory calculator to a multi-class, object oriented terminal game.
For his first group project, he worked with a team of fellow students to create a retail Point of Sale system incorporating a dynamic inventory list acessible from an employee login.
He has humble beginnings, but through his commitment to diligence and constant practice, DVD shows a lot of promise as a future software engineer.

HOMETOWN

DVD was born in Cleveland, OH, under a Pisces sign, before almost immediately transplanting to Pennsylvania.
They lived for a time in Harrisburg, and later Pittsburgh, before finally settling in Troy, MI.
Michigan will remain a state of beauty;Troy was anything but, with ten square miles of gridded neighborhoods and strip plazas.
It was DVD's hometown, but while their peers embraced the town as their forever homes, DVD reacted to it by forever leaving.
They have since lived in Seoul, ROK; Chicago, IL; and now Jersey City, NJ, part of the NYC metro.
