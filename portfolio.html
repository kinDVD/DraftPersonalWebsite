1. DAD's Coffee Shop

By DeAngelo Robinson, Afseen Salam, and DVD Goodwin

A POS (Point of Sale) system for a retail food service business.
Complete with ordering menu, receipt functionality with tip,
an inventory management system, employee login, colored terminal display, and audio ambience.

2. Car Lab

By Wren Grasley, Afseen Salam, and DVD Goodwin

A car sales program, complete with inventory management capabilities based on elements of each car.

3. Land the Baja

By DVD Goodwin, original concept by Grand Circus

A complete mod of an assessment problem. The original task: create a list of astronauts with traitor booleans
If the traitor boolean is set to true when the Baja attempts to lan, the baja crashes.
The player's goal is to jettison the traitors, but to do so, the player must first figure out
who the traitors are. Run drills to gain insight into who the traitors are,
and you just might land this.
